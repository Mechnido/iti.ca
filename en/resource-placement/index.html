Why place your trust in us?

At ITI, we connect people, not just wires. We leverage human intelligence, not just artificial intelligence.

That’s why we offer human solutions to resource placement and talent acquisition. Our team of specialists provides personalized support both to candidates looking to embark on a passionate career in IT and companies looking to hire the best IT professionals.

  • We recruit for companies

  • We place IT specialists

  • We guarantee exceptional client support

You are a candidate

Looking for a stimulating job in the IT industry? We know there are more personal and professional paths than there are APIs. Our team takes the time to understand your aspirations and propose contracts that are in line with your ambitions.

  • Major IT projects

    Take part in exciting projects for major companies and organizations in Quebec and the rest of Canada.

  • Be valued for your expertise

    Our recruitment specialists will highlight your skills.

  • Professional support and frequent follow-up

    We keep in touch to make sure you’re satisfied with your position.

  • Career development

    Advance your career through numerous mandates for the same organization, or via different companies.

  • Networking

    Our ITI Culture and Innovation committee hosts coffee chats and IT webinars.

  • Competitive pay and benefits

    Whatever your placement, ITI provides group insurance, sick days and holiday pay.

You are an employer

Need talent, now? We’ll recruit the best candidates for your company, despite the IT labour shortage. Our IT experts have been supporting companies for more than 30 years, so we understand the challenges you face.

  • IT recruitment specialists

    IT is in our DNA. We know how to identify the required skills and qualifications to meet your needs. We speak the languages of technology and business.

  • Qualified resource bank

    Our IT experts are ready to join your teams quickly.

  • Satisfaction guaranteed

    We guarantee our candidates’ skills and ability to fulfill your needs.

  • We handle everything, free of charge

    We take care of the entire recruitment process, from finding qualified candidates to conducting interviews.

  • Regular follow-up

    Your satisfaction is our priority. That’s why we ensure your resource is meeting your expectations.

  • Wide-ranging service offer

    From our placement service (permanent or temporary; short, medium or long term) to value-added service (deployment, migration or move), we make your IT projects happen.

  • Certified and recognized

    ITI meets ISO 9001:2015 standards and ranks 7th out of Canada’s 100 best IT solution suppliers according to CDN 2021.
